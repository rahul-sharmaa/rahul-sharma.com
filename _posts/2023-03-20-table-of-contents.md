Education must go beyond knowledge delivery and address global challenges such as climate change, inequality, and sustainable development.

Artificial Intelligence can accelerate progress toward the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

#### Key Areas of Impact

- Smart infrastructure (SDG 9)  
- Climate action (SDG 13)  
- Responsible innovation (SDG 12)  
- Quality education (SDG 4)  

#### Role of Universities

- Embed sustainability into curriculum  
- Promote ethical AI  
- Encourage interdisciplinary research  
- Develop socially responsible graduates  

> The future of education lies at the intersection of AI, sustainability, and global responsibility.

Universities must lead this transformation to remain relevant in a rapidly changing world.
