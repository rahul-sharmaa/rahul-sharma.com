Generation Z is entering a world shaped by automation, artificial intelligence, and rapid technological change. The skills required for success are evolving dramatically.

#### Core Skill Areas

- Critical thinking and problem-solving  
- AI and digital literacy  
- Interdisciplinary thinking  
- Creativity and innovation  
- Emotional intelligence and communication  

#### Why It Matters

Traditional knowledge alone is no longer sufficient. Students must learn:

- How to learn continuously  
- How to work with AI systems  
- How to solve complex global problems  

> The future belongs to those who can adapt faster than technology evolves.

Universities must redesign curricula to embed these competencies across all programs.
